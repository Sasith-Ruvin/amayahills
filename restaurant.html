Restaurants

The Sri Lankan culinary scene makes our island home a food paradise. Let the award-winning chefs at Amaya Hills, Kandy take you on a gastronomic adventure while they create fresh flavours to complement the stunning views. Beyond the cool climate wines and ethereal misty mountains, taste the very best of the island from local to western and international cuisine here in the hill capital. Discover the best of what leading restaurants in Kandy have to offer at Amaya Hills.

The Gourmet

The Gourmet at Amaya Hills is one of the best restaurants in Kandy, serving some of the finest dishes prepared to perfection, using fresh, local ingredients. Take a seat indoors or better yet, out by the terrace where you will feel transported to a whole new world as you dine with a spectacular view of the mountains. The interior of the restaurant is equally charming with traditional motifs and Kandyan paintings, creating a delightfully unique atmosphere

Tea Lounge

Sit back with a warm cup of pure Ceylon tea at Tea Lounge, the resort’s quaint yet modern tea lounge and spot for the best High Tea in Kandy. With hues of reds and blues depicting the colours of the Kandyan era and large floor to ceiling windows opening out to the terrace, it’s the ideal place to work, read a book or just relax. Choose from a variety of delicious gourmet cakes, croissants and a choice of Sri Lankan pastries
