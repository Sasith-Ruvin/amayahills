EXPERIENCE AMAYA HILLS




The Amaya Hills in Kandy, which are part of the cultural triangle, include views of the Heerassagala forest. The resort is located 6.4 kilometers from the center of Kandy and 117.5 kilometers from Bandaranaike International Airport. This stately palace in the hills, which is regarded as one of the greatest hotels in Kandy, is located in the coziest of settings amidst natural surroundings. Enjoy the royal luxury that came from Sri Lanka's last monarchy while taking in the breathtaking views of the city from the forest-covered highlands of Heerassagala and the endless Knuckles mountain range.
