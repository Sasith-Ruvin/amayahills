Resorts

ICONIC RESORTS THAT SPARK YOUR IMAGINATION

Amaya Beach


YOUR BEACHFRONT SANCTUARY


Located on the dreamy shores of the Indian Ocean, the stunning Amaya Beach, is the pearl of the iconic Pasikudah bay.


With 125 contemporarily furnished rooms and suites, this beach haven offers and idyllic environment to relax and recharge.

Amaya Kuda Rah


ELEVATED LUXURY


Explore the underwater world at the nearby Kuda Rah thila, set sail on a cruise with your loved one or experience a massage at our renowned spa.


With 51 deluxe villas, 3 restaurants and bars, contemporary facilities and exceptional service, Amaya Kuda Rah is the most luxurious property in the Malsectiones.

Amaya Lake


INSPIRED BY NATURE


A beautiful lakefront resort with glistening views of the Kandalama lake.A forest-laden pathway leads to the enchanting seclusion of Amaya Lake.


Nestled along the shores of the Kandalama reservoir and within close proximity to a number of local attractions including the UNESCO world heritage site Sigiriya, Amaya Lake is one of the most coveted Dambulla hotels. The resort is a 3.5 hour (86 miles / 140km) drive from the Bandaranaike International Airport.
